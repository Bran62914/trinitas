About Trinitas

Trinitas is a classical christian school which teaches the students to love and follow God first then school second. It is k5-12th grade school. At trinitas we encourage students to read classical literature. Every quarter each student is assigened one Boni Liberi and chosses one of there own. Each student must finish their Boni Liberi and write a short summary on it.

Trinitas is also sports centered. Our schools team is the knights!
